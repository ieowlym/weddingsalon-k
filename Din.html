ABOUT OUR SALON

Wedding salon "Diamond" in Almaty offers the widest selection of luxury wedding dresses from leading Italian and American brands. Opened in 2018, we continue to delight thousands of our brides. In our salon there is a pleasant and cozy atmosphere, each bride is guaranteed high quality service, individual approach and attention.

To date, the collection of wedding dresses of the famous brand Diamond because of their high cost were available only to the elect. In 2018, in order to make Diamond's luxury available to everyone, the company created a collection that embodies the dreams of any bride — these are classic lush dresses, touchingly romantic ampers, strict A-silhouettes and sexy tight fish silhouettes. And what is most important in the collection is a combination of cost of clothes and quality of tailoring and used fabrics.

Preserving the tradition of the brand, Malinelli wedding dresses are always committed to the idea of quality in everything that the company represents. Starting this year, visiting the salons representing the Malinelli trademark, brides will undoubtedly be able to find the outfit of their dreams, regardless of budget. We work daily from 10 a.m. to 7 p.m. or until the last client.

You can find our address and location map in the "Contact" section. Diamond Wedding Salon in Almaty, Kazakhstan.
